Privacy Policy

Storyo · Last updated: April 2026
Storyo is a personal bedtime story app for families. We built it with a simple principle: your voice recordings and your children's data belong to you, not to us. This policy explains exactly what the app does and does not do with your data.
🎙️
Microphone Access

Storyo requests microphone permission solely to allow parents and grandparents to record bedtime stories in their own voice.

Audio is recorded directly on your device and saved locally. It is never streamed, transmitted or uploaded to any server by Storyo.

✅ Your voice recordings never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to export and share a story yourself.
💾
Data Collection

Storyo does not collect any personal data. The app has no backend server, no database, no analytics, and no user accounts.

Everything the app stores — recordings, stories, illustrations, settings and your PIN — is saved locally on your device using IndexedDB (browser storage).

✅ Storyo has no servers. There is no company database containing your name, email, location or usage data.
🤖
AI Illustrations & API Keys

Storyo optionally generates illustrations using third-party AI services. If you choose to use this feature, you provide your own API key from one of the supported providers:

Anthropic (Claude SVG) · OpenAI (GPT Image 1) · Google (Nano Banana)

Your API key is stored only on your device and is sent directly from your device to the AI provider's API when generating an illustration. Storyo never sees, stores or transmits your API key to any other party.

The AI provider receives only the illustration prompt text you write. Please refer to the privacy policies of Anthropic, OpenAI and Google for how they handle API requests.

✅ Using AI illustrations is entirely optional. The app works fully without any API key.
👶
Children's Privacy

Storyo is designed for use by families, including young children. Because the app collects no personal data and has no network communication of its own, there is no risk of children's data being shared with third parties through Storyo.

The app contains no advertising, no tracking, no analytics and no social features.

✅ Storyo is fully compliant with children's privacy standards including COPPA and GDPR-K.
🚫
Advertising & Tracking

Storyo contains no advertising of any kind. We do not use any advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or analytics libraries.

We do not share any data with advertisers or data brokers because we do not collect any data to share.

💳
In-App Purchases

Storyo offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to unlock the full version. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. Storyo does not process, store or have access to any payment or billing information.

The purchase status (purchased / not purchased) is verified directly with Google Play on your device.

📋
Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Since Storyo collects no personal data, changes to this policy will generally only reflect changes in the app's features rather than changes in data handling practices.
